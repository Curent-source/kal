Lab History

Establishment of the School of Engineering Science at the University of Osaka

1961.03

Establishment of the Department of Control Engineering in the School of Engineering Science

1962.03

Yoshifumi Sakurai Laboratory Established

Retirement of Professor Sakurai

1983.03

After retirement, Professor Sakurai

  • Became Professor Emeritus of the University of Osaka
  • Moved to Setsunan University and served as President, later moved to Osaka Institute of Technology and served as President
  • Received The Order of the Sacred Treasure, Gold and Silver Star

Seiji Inokuchi Laboratory Established

1983.04

The Department of Control Engineering renamed as the Department of Systems Engineering

1992.04

The Departments of Control Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Biotechnology merge into the Department of Systems Science

1997.04

The former Department of Control Engineering is reorganized into the Electronic Systems Science Course

Retirement of Professor Inokuchi

2003.03

After retirement, Professor Inokouchi

  • Became Professor Emeritus of the University of Osaka
  • Moved to Hiroshima International University, then later to Takarazuka University
  • Received The Order of the Sacred Treasure, Gold Rays with Neck Ribbon

Kosuke Sato Laboratory Established

2003.04

The Electronic Systems Science Course renamed as the Intelligent Systems Science Course

2014.04

Retirement of Professor Sato

2025.03

After retirement, Professor Sato

  • Became Professor Emeritus of the University of Osaka
  • Moved to Otemon Gakuin University and served as the Dean of the Faculty of Science and Engineering

Daisuke Iwai Laboratory Established

2025.04

  • Professor : Daisuke Iwai
  • Associate Professor : Tomoya Nakamura
  • Assistant Professor : Takashi Ameesaka
  • Administration : Yuko Korekuni
