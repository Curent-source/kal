Join us

We are seeking highly motivated students who are enthusiastic about pursuing novel XR technologies that have the potential to revolutionize society.

Prospective graduate students

Please consult the Admissions Assistance Desk (AAD). We do not accept research students. Only those who pass the entrance examination can join our lab. Most international students who join our lab are supported by a MEXT scholarship (Embassy recommendation).

Exchange programs

We accept students through university-wide exchange programs (OUSSEP, iEXPO, FrontierLab, Maple).
