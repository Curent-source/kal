CVPR 2026にて口頭発表およびポスター発表を行いました

2026.06.06

CVPR 2026にて、2025年度修士卒 河野が口頭発表＋ポスター発表を、特任研究員S 河内、OB 井元がポスター発表 (Findings) をそれぞれ行いました。

（なお、写真は河野と博士学生 三浦です）

  • Takumi Kawano, Kohei Miura, Daisuke Iwai, “Breaking the Scalability Limit of Multi-Projector Calibration with Embedded Cameras,” In Proceedings of The IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR), pp. 21573-21582 (2026). (Oral) [詳細]
  • Hodaka Kawachi, Tomoya Nakamura, Hiroaki Santo, SaiKiran K. Tedla, Trevor D. Canham, Yasushi Yagi, and Michael S. Brown, “Towards Imperceptible Watermarking Via Environment Illumination for Consumer Cameras,” The IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR) 2026 Finding Track, Denver, Colorado, USA, Jun. 2026.
  • Yuichiro Takeuchi, Yusuke Imoto, Shunya Kato, “Ninja Codes: Neurally Generated Fiducial Markers for Stealthy 6-DoF Tracking,” Proceedings of the IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR) Findings, 2026, pp. 6880-6889.
